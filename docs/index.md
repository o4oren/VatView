**Live VATSIM tracking for pilots and aviation enthusiasts.**

VatView is a free mobile app for iOS and Android that shows you live [VATSIM](https://vatsim.net) traffic on an interactive map — pilots, ATC controllers, airports, events, and more.

---

## Features

- **Live map** — pilots and ATC updated every 20 seconds
- **Track yourself and friends** — enter your VATSIM CID and a friends list to highlight your flights and see friends' markers in distinct colors
- **Type-specific aircraft icons** — 50+ aircraft types with accurate silhouettes
- **Real airspace boundaries** — FIR, UIR, and TRACON polygons
- **Airport detail** — staffed ATC positions and traffic count badges
- **Progressive disclosure panels** — tap any marker for full flight and ATC info
- **Events and bookings** — upcoming VATSIM events and ATC schedules
- **METAR weather** — search any airport for current weather
- **Light and dark themes**
- **Landscape orientation support**

## What is VATSIM?

[VATSIM](https://vatsim.net) (Virtual Air Traffic Simulation Network) is a free online platform where flight simulation enthusiasts fly and control air traffic together in real time. VatView lets you watch the network live — even if you're not flying.
