# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

VatView is a free mobile app for tracking live VATSIM network activity. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

---

## What Data We Collect

**VatView does not require an account and does not collect any personally identifiable information directly.**

### Crash Reports
VatView uses [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/products/crashlytics) to collect crash reports when the app encounters an error. This may include:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Stack trace of the crash

This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

### Analytics
VatView uses [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/products/analytics) to collect anonymous usage data, including:
- Which screens are viewed
- Which features are used (e.g. map filters, detail panels)

No personally identifiable information is included in analytics events.

### Map Data
VatView uses [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com) to render the map. Google's standard privacy policy applies to map tile requests. No user location data is collected or transmitted by VatView.

### VATSIM Data
Live VATSIM network data (pilot callsigns, positions, ATC controllers) is fetched from the public [VATSIM data feed](https://data.vatsim.net). This data is public and not collected or stored by VatView.

---

## Data Storage

All app preferences and cached data (map region, filter settings, airport database) are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to VatView servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

VatView uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Maps](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [VATSIM](https://vatsim.net/privacy)

---

## Children's Privacy

VatView does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
